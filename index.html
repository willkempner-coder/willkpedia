Will Kempner (born June 12, 1993) is an American editor, filmmaker, artist, and producer. Kempner has worked across television, fashion campaigns, branded and corporate media, and independent film. His portfolio includes directing and editing narrative shorts and features, as well as post-production work on series for HBO and Disney+.[1][2][3][4]

Biography [edit]

Kempner began working in post-production in the late 2000s and later built a cross-disciplinary practice spanning editing, assistant editing, producing, and copywriting. He has served as lead assistant editor and associate editor across both seasons of HBO's Random Acts of Flyness (2018; 2022) and worked as an editor on Disney+'s Growing Up (2022).[1][2][3]

In advertising and fashion, Kempner has edited and produced work for Telfar, including New Models (Season 0 and Season 1, 2023-present?). He was also part of the team (with Colin Garland and Ben Townsend) that won the 2019 Tribeca Film Festival Snapchat Story Contest.[5][6][7]
